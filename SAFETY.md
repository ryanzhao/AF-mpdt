# Safety Notice

This project involves risks associated with high current, high temperature, vacuum systems, plasma, and mechanical structures.

The materials in this repository are provided only for research, education, and reference purposes. They are not a substitute for professional engineering review, laboratory safety procedures, or applicable laws and regulations.

People without relevant experience should not directly reproduce the experiments, assemblies, or tests described or implied by this project.

Any testing must be performed only in an appropriate laboratory environment with qualified supervision, suitable personal and facility safety protections, emergency stop systems, and proper electrical isolation.

Control panels, power supplies, vacuum chambers, and thruster tests should be inspected by people with relevant experience before use.
